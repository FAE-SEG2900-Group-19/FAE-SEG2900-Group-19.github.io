Our story

FAE has been created by six students of the University of Ottawa. They struggled with getting the time for online deliveries. So, they thought of something that will not only solve the issue but also could be trust-worthy. And that's how was born FAE, and our service Dronelivery a unique way to get your deliveries by drone.
